Brand Story
We are aware of the wide gap that exists...

.....between industries and the discovery networks in Africa, and then started to look for the efficient means to bring about productive synergy.

After listening to over 50 Pitch Deck at CBN sponsored Entrepreneurship Development Programme in 2015, we discovered that majority of the entrepreneurs are replicative in their orientations, approaches, and practices.

This relatively non-existence of innovative entrepreneurs resulting from lack of an organized innovation ecosystem to facilitate the commercialization of innovative discoveries in Africa amplified our concerns.

Keeping eyes on technological...

...innovations and experimental discoveries trend around the world through patent data base and other IP assets variables, the founder alongside practiced in agric value-chain and commodities export market to understand the degree of domestic utilization of agricultural output for real sector businesses and industries expansion.

Upon learning the gaps and the possible means to drive innovation in Africa, we opted to set up a technology-based channel to help the academic researchers, inventors, creators, venture startups, and corporations to do better at product creation, investment mobilization and global value-chain participation. Then, Marketleag was birth to give rise to the emergence of innovative solutions, corporate venturing, and organized IP market in Africa.

Marketleag, a brand of the Market Leadership Global Co., is democratizing technology development, innovation process and intellectual property management through digital collaboration and multi-actor alliances.
